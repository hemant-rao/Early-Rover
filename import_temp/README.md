# Solar Alarm

Solar Alarm is an interactive, location-aware astronomical sun-tracking alarm clock combined with a live weather and AQI monitoring station. Stay synchronized with the natural cycles of your current environment.

## Core Features
*   **Astro-Sync Alarms**: Real-time sunrise and sunset tracking based on your exact location.
*   **Live Weather & AQI**: Intelligent weather forecasting and air quality index monitoring.
*   **3D Sky Visualization**: Dynamic visualization of the sky and sun position using WebGL.
*   **Location Management**: Add, manage, and monitor multiple locations.
*   **Adaptive Theme**: Day/Night theming based on time and environmental state.
