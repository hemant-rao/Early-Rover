## Full App Description

Early Rover is an intelligent alarm and travel assistant designed to help you wake up and stay on time wherever life takes you.

Whether you are traveling by train, bus, metro, flight, or simply want reminders for sunrise, sunset, sleep cycles, or daily schedules, Early Rover ensures you never miss an important moment.

With advanced GPS tracking, destination alerts, smart wake-up technology, and customizable alarms, Early Rover becomes your reliable companion for travel, productivity, and everyday life.
